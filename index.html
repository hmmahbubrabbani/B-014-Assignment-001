Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is open to software developers, engineers, students, startup founders, designers, and anyone interested in technology and innovation.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All sessions will be recorded. Pro ticket holders receive lifetime access to the recordings.

Can I participate in the hackathon?

Yes! Every registered attendee is eligible to join the 48-hour hackathon and compete for exciting prizes. Teams can have 2-5 members.

Do I need to bring my laptop?

Yes. We recommend bringing your own laptop for workshops, coding sessions, and the hackathon.

Is food included with the ticket?

Yes. Lunch, snacks, and coffee will be provided on all three conference days.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes. Tickets can be transferred to another attendee up to 7 days before the event.

How can I become a sponsor or exhibitor?

Companies interested in sponsoring or exhibiting at DevConf 2026 can contact our partnership team through the Sponsor page.
